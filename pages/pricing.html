Common Questions

What does "early access" mean?

We're live and functional, but actively improving based on real user feedback. Early access partners get to influence what features we prioritize and get preferential pricing.

How much does it cost?

We're flexible for early adopters. Reach out and we'll find a price that works for your venue size and needs. Beta partners will get locked-in rates below our future standard pricing.

What if I need a feature you don't have yet?

Tell us! As an early access partner, your feature requests go to the top of the roadmap. We're building this with real operators, not hypothetical users.

Is there a contract?

No long-term lock-ins. Month-to-month for early access partners. We earn your business every month by being useful.
