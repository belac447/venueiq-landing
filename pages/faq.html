What are the 4 modules?

Does VenueIQ integrate with my POS system?
+
Currently, VenueIQ works via CSV import from any POS system. Export your sales data, upload to VenueIQ, and we handle the rest. Direct API integrations are on the roadmap, and early access partners help prioritize which POS systems we integrate with first.
How does real-time cost tracking work?
+
When you process a supplier invoice in StockIQ, ingredient prices automatically update across all recipes that use those ingredients. Historical cost snapshots let you see exactly when and how costs changed. You can set alerts to notify you when recipe costs increase beyond your threshold (e.g., 5%).
What is BCG matrix analysis?
+
BCG matrix is a menu engineering framework that classifies dishes into 4 categories:

Stars: High popularity, high profitability - promote these!
Cash Cows: Lower popularity but high profit - keep them for profit margins.
Question Marks: High popularity but low profit - reprice or restructure.
Dogs: Low popularity, low profit - consider removing from menu.

VenueIQ automatically calculates this using your sales data and recipe costs.

Multi-venue support is coming soon. Current early access partners with multiple locations can track each venue separately and we're prioritizing this feature based on demand. Let us know if this is critical for you.

Is VenueIQ cloud-based or installed software?
+
Cloud-based. Access from any device with a web browser - desktop, tablet, or phone. No installation required. Your data is securely stored and backed up automatically.

+
Yes. VenueIQ uses industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage. Your recipe data, supplier information, and sales analytics are private and not shared with third parties. Regular backups ensure your data is never lost.
What browsers are supported?

VenueIQ works on all modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Mobile browsers are fully supported too, though some complex data entry tasks are easier on desktop.

Absolutely. Your data is yours. You can export recipes, costs, inventory, and sales data to CSV format anytime. No lock-in, no proprietary formats.
