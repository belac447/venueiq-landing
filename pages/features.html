CostIQ
Recipe Costing Intelligence

Track recipe costs in real-time with automatic updates when ingredient prices change. Get accurate cost data for better pricing decisions.

  • ✓
    Real-Time Costing - Automatic cost calculations that update with ingredient price changes
  • ✓
    Cost History Tracking - Historical snapshots show how costs have changed over time
  • ✓
    Recipe Management - Easy-to-use interface for building and editing recipes
  • ✓
    Menu Performance Analysis - BCG matrix analysis to identify high and low performing items
  • ✓
    Profit Margin Tracking - See profit margins per dish alongside cost percentages

Real Use Case

"CostIQ showed our salmon dish had crept to 41% food cost—way above our 28% target. We were selling 85/week at $24. Repriced to $26.50, kept selling the same volume. That single menu item now generates an extra $1,800/month in gross profit."

"RevenueIQ showed our 'premium' steak was our worst performer—low sales, high cost, kitchen hated prepping it. Meanwhile, our pork belly sold 3x more with better margins. Cut the steak, expanded pork offerings, simplified BOH workflow. Added $2,400/month and reduced stress."
